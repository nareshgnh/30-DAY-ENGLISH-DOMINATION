**CONFIDENCE BOOST STATEMENT:**

Day 19! You've mastered answering questions - today you master ASKING them. "Do you have any questions for us?" is your opportunity to shine. Great questions prove you're serious, thoughtful, and genuinely interested. Weak or no questions? Red flag. Senior candidates ask intelligent questions. Today you learn to ask questions that impress interviewers AND help you evaluate if the company is right for you. This is power. Let's dominate!

---

**BREATHING EXERCISE (2 min):**

1. **Curiosity Breath:**
   - Breathe in: "I am genuinely curious about this role"
   - Breathe out: "My questions show my interest and intelligence"
   - Repeat 3 times

2. **Confidence Breath:**
   - Breathe in: "I'm interviewing them too"
   - Breathe out: "I choose the right company for me"
   - Repeat 3 times

3. **Clarity Breath:**
   - Breathe in: "I ask clear, intelligent questions"
   - Breathe out: "I learn what I need to know"
   - Repeat 3 times

---

**WARM-UP SENTENCES (5 min):**

1. "Can you tell me more about the team structure and how collaboration works?"

2. "What would success look like for this role in the first 90 days?"

3. "How does the company support professional growth and learning?"

4. "What are the biggest challenges the team is currently facing?"

5. "Can you describe the typical career path for someone in this role?"

---

**TONGUE TWISTERS (3 min):**

1. "Could you clarify the company's current culture change concerning cross-team collaboration?"

2. "What challenges characterize the current quarter for the quality assurance team?"

3. "How does the organization prioritize professional development opportunities?"

---

**QUICK GRAMMAR RECALL (5 min):**

**Today's Focus:**
- Question formation: "Can you...", "Could you...", "Would you..."
- "What would success look like..." (conditional)
- "How does the team..." (present simple for general processes)
