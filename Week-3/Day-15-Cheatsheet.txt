## ‚ö° TOP 5 GRAMMAR RULES

**Rule 1:** Use Present Perfect for ongoing experience
- ‚úÖ "I have been working at HCL for 6 years"
- ‚úÖ "I have gained extensive experience in automation"

**Rule 2:** Use Past Simple for completed past actions
- ‚úÖ "I worked at TCS before joining HCL"
- ‚úÖ "I completed my B.Tech in 2018"

**Rule 3:** Use Present Continuous for current actions
- ‚úÖ "I am currently working on automation frameworks"
- ‚úÖ "I am looking for opportunities in product companies"

**Rule 4:** Structure "Tell me about yourself" as Present ‚Üí Past ‚Üí Future
- ‚úÖ "I am currently [role]. I have [experience]. I am looking for [goal]."
- ‚úÖ "I work as [role]. Previously I worked at [company]. I want to [future goal]."

**Rule 5:** Use confident but humble professional language
- ‚úÖ "I am confident that my experience aligns with this role"
- ‚úÖ "I believe I can contribute effectively to your team"
