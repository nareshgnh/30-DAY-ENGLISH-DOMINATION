## ‚ö° TOP 5 SMALL TALK RULES

**Rule 1:** Use Comment + Question formula
- ‚úÖ "Great weather today, isn't it? How was your weekend?"
- ‚úÖ "Nice office space! How long have you been working here?"

**Rule 2:** FISH topics are safe (Food, Interests, Sports, Hobbies)
- ‚úÖ Weekend plans, hobbies, weather, food, sports, technology, commute
- ‚ùå Salary, politics, religion, gossip

**Rule 3:** Active listening = Ask follow-up questions
- ‚úÖ Them: "I like cycling" ‚Üí You: "Oh nice! How long have you been cycling?"
- ‚ùå Them: "I like cycling" ‚Üí You: "Cool. Do you like coding?"

**Rule 4:** Small talk BEFORE business, especially in interviews
- ‚úÖ 2-3 min rapport building ‚Üí then technical questions
- ‚ùå Jumping straight to "Tell me about yourself"

**Rule 5:** End conversations warmly
- ‚úÖ "Thanks so much for your time! Looking forward to hearing from you!"
- ‚ùå Abrupt: "OK bye" or awkwardly lingering

## ‚ùå TOP 5 MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. ‚ùå No small talk, straight to business ‚Üí ‚úÖ Build rapport first 2-3 minutes

2. ‚ùå Asking unsafe topics (salary, politics) ‚Üí ‚úÖ Stick to FISH topics

## üìù QUICK FORMULAS

**OPENING FORMULA:**
[Friendly greeting] + [Comment/Observation] + [Open question]
Example: "Hi! Thanks for meeting me. The office looks amazing! How long have you been working here?"

**FOLLOW-UP FORMULA:**
[Acknowledge their answer] + [Related follow-up question]
Example: Them: "I like hiking." You: "Oh that's cool! Where do you usually go hiking?"

**CLOSING FORMULA:**
[Thank them] + [Express enthusiasm] + [Mention next steps if relevant]
Example: "Thanks so much for your time today! I really enjoyed our conversation. Looking forward to hearing about next steps!"
