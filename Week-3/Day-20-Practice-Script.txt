### SESSION 1: TOUGH QUESTIONS RAPID FIRE (40 min)

**ChatGPT Setup Prompt:**

```
Act as a tough interviewer asking pressure questions rapidly.

Ask me:
- Salary expectations
- Biggest weakness
- Why leaving current job
- Biggest failure
- Why should we hire you
- Gap in resume
- How you handle criticism
- Working with difficult people
- Overqualified concerns
- Current salary

Fire questions quickly. Evaluate:
- Do I stay calm or get flustered?
- Are answers honest but strategic?
- Grammar accuracy under pressure
- Do I turn negatives into positives?

After 40 min, rate my composure and answers (0-10).

Start: "Let's get to some direct questions. What are your salary expectations?"
```

---

### SESSION 2: SALARY NEGOTIATION PRACTICE (30 min)

**ChatGPT Setup Prompt:**

```
Practice salary negotiation conversation.

You're the hiring manager. We discuss:
- My salary expectations
- Your budget constraints
- Benefits and overall package
- My justification for range
- Flexibility and negotiation

Teach me to negotiate confidently but reasonably.

After 30 min, rate my negotiation skills (0-10).
```

---

### SESSION 3: WEAKNESS & FAILURE STORIES (30 min)

**ChatGPT Setup Prompt:**

```
Focus on weakness and failure questions.

Test if I:
- Pick REAL weaknesses (not "I work too hard")
- Show self-awareness
- Explain improvement
- Own failures without excuses
- Demonstrate learning

After 30 min, evaluate: Do I handle these questions maturely?
```

---

### SESSION 4: FULL PRESSURE INTERVIEW (20 min)

**ChatGPT Setup Prompt:**

```
Conduct a 20-minute high-pressure interview with:
- Tough questions
- Challenging follow-ups
- Skeptical tone
- Testing my composure

Evaluate: Can I stay calm and professional under maximum pressure?

Give final rating: Am I ready for tough interviews?
```
