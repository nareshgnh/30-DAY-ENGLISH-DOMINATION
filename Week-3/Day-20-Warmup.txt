**BREATHING EXERCISE (2 min):**

1. **Pressure Management Breath:**
   - Breathe in: "I remain calm under pressure"
   - Hold: "Tough questions don't shake me"
   - Breathe out: "I answer with confidence and honesty"
   - Repeat 3 times

2. **Honesty with Strategy Breath:**
   - Breathe in: "I own my story"
   - Breathe out: "I turn challenges into strengths"
   - Repeat 3 times

3. **Composure Breath:**
   - Breathe in: "I pause and think before answering"
   - Breathe out: "I deliver thoughtful, strategic answers"
   - Repeat 3 times

---

**WARM-UP SENTENCES (5 min):**

1. "My expected salary range is between 20 to 25 LPA, based on my experience and market standards."

2. "My biggest weakness used to be public speaking, but I've actively worked on it through practice."

3. "I'm looking to leave my current role because I'm seeking opportunities to work on larger-scale systems."

4. "That gap in my resume was due to personal circumstances, but I used that time productively to upskill."

5. "My failure taught me the importance of planning and communication, which I've improved significantly since then."

---

**TONGUE TWISTERS (3 min):**

1. "I professionally proposed a proportionate performance-based pay package of twenty-five lakhs annually."

2. "While I was working on the critical project, weaknesses were identified which I subsequently strengthened systematically."

3. "Though the transition period presented problems, I prepared properly through personal professional development."

---

**QUICK GRAMMAR RECALL (5 min):**

**Today's Focus:**
- Conditional: "If I were in that situation again, I would..." (hypothetical past)
- Past simple for experiences: "I left because...", "I learned..."
- Present perfect for improvements: "I have worked on...", "I have improved..."
