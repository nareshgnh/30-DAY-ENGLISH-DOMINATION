### SECTION 2: FULL INTERVIEW STRUCTURE

**TYPICAL 60-MINUTE INTERVIEW BREAKDOWN:**

**Phase 1: OPENING (5 min)**
- Greeting and small talk
- "Tell me about yourself"

**Phase 2: TECHNICAL QUESTIONS (20 min)**
- Explain your framework/tools
- Technical problem-solving
- Design decisions

**Phase 3: BEHAVIORAL QUESTIONS (20 min)**
- STAR stories (challenge, conflict, failure, leadership, achievement)
- Teamwork and collaboration examples

**Phase 4: PRESSURE QUESTIONS (10 min)**
- Salary expectations
- Weakness/gaps
- Why leaving current job

**Phase 5: YOUR QUESTIONS (5 min)**
- Ask 3-5 intelligent questions

**Phase 6: CLOSING (2 min)**
- Thank you and next steps
- Final small talk

### SECTION 4: WEEK 3 KEY GRAMMAR PATTERNS

**Interview Basics (Day 15):**
- "I am currently working as..."
- "I have been working in [field] for [X] years"
- "Before joining [company], I worked at..."

**Technical Explanations (Day 16):**
- "The framework uses [tool]"
- "This allows us to [benefit]"
- "To put it simply, [analogy]"

**STAR Stories (Day 17):**
- "The situation was that..."
- "My task was to..."
- "I implemented/created/solved..."
- "The result was [outcome with numbers]"

**Small Talk (Day 18):**
- "How was your weekend?"
- "Nice weather, isn't it?"
- "That sounds interesting! Tell me more."

**Asking Questions (Day 19):**
- "Could you tell me about..."
- "What would success look like..."
- "How does the team handle..."

**Pressure Questions (Day 20):**
- "My expectation is [range]"
- "My weakness used to be [X], but I have improved..."
- "I'm leaving because I'm seeking [positive goal]"
