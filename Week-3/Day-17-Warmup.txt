**WARM-UP SENTENCES (5 min):**

Read each sentence aloud 3 times. These are STAR framework sentences.

1. "The situation was that our test suite was taking 3 hours to run, blocking daily releases."

2. "My task was to reduce the execution time by at least 50 percent without compromising coverage."

3. "The action I took was implementing parallel test execution using Pytest-xdist across 5 containers."

4. "The result was that we reduced runtime from 3 hours to 45 minutes, enabling faster releases."

5. "This experience taught me how to identify bottlenecks and implement scalable solutions under pressure."

**Today's Preview (Day 17 - Behavioral Questions/STAR):**
- Focus: Past Simple tense for completed actions ("I implemented...", "I solved...")
- Focus: Past Perfect for actions before other past actions ("I had identified the issue before the meeting")
- Focus: Storytelling sequence words ("First...", "Then...", "As a result...")
- Focus: Showing impact with numbers and metrics

**Key Grammar for Today:**
- "I faced [situation]" (past simple)
- "I had to [task]" (past simple with 'to' for obligation)
- "I implemented [solution]" (past simple action)
- "This resulted in [outcome]" (past simple result)
