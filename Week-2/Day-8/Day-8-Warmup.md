## BREATHING EXERCISE (2 min):

**Exercise 1: Power Breathing**
- Inhale for 4 counts: "I am becoming fluent"
- Hold for 4 counts: "My speaking improves daily"
- Exhale for 6 counts: "I speak with confidence and clarity"
- Repeat 3 times

**Exercise 2: Articulation Breath**
- Deep breath in
- Exhale while saying: "However, therefore, moreover, furthermore, additionally"
- Focus on crisp pronunciation of each word
- Repeat 2 times

**Exercise 3: Connector Flow**
- Inhale deeply
- Exhale while saying: "First, I analyzed the issue. Then, I created a solution. Finally, I implemented the fix."
- Feel the smooth flow between ideas
- Repeat 2 times

## WARM-UP SENTENCES (5 min):

Read these sentences aloud 3 times each, focusing on smooth transitions:

1. "I have experience in manual testing. However, I've primarily focused on automation for the past three years."

2. "Our framework uses Playwright for UI testing. Additionally, we've integrated it with our CI/CD pipeline for continuous testing."

3. "The initial approach wasn't working well. Therefore, I suggested refactoring the entire test architecture."

4. "Manual testing is essential for exploratory work. On the other hand, automation is crucial for regression testing."

5. "First, I identify the critical user flows. Then, I prioritize them based on business impact. Finally, I automate the highest-priority scenarios."

**Key Connectors for Today:**
- However (contrast)
- Therefore (result)
- Moreover/Furthermore (addition)
- On the other hand (alternative view)
- In addition (adding information)
