## ‚ö° TOP 5 GRAMMAR RULES

**Rule 1:** Use "IN MY OPINION" / "I BELIEVE" instead of "I think" in interviews
- ‚úÖ "In my opinion, automation provides the best ROI for regression testing"
- ‚úÖ "I believe that test maintainability is more important than coverage"

**Rule 2:** Use "FROM MY EXPERIENCE" to establish credibility
- ‚úÖ "From my experience, flaky tests are usually caused by poor wait strategies"
- ‚úÖ "In my previous role, I reduced test execution time by 70%"

**Rule 3:** Use "I'M CONFIDENT THAT" instead of "maybe" / "I'll try"
- ‚úÖ "I'm confident that I can deliver the framework within 3 months"
- ‚úÖ "Without a doubt, my skills align well with this senior role"

**Rule 4:** Use "IT SEEMS" / "PERHAPS" when softening or uncertain
- ‚úÖ "It seems to me that the issue might be related to timing"
- ‚úÖ "Perhaps we could consider starting with API testing first"

**Rule 5:** Use "I'D ARGUE THAT" for strong professional opinions
- ‚úÖ "I'd argue that 70-80% coverage is more practical than 100%"
- ‚úÖ "Based on what I've seen, early QA involvement reduces overall defects"

## üíº WORKPLACE PHRASES (Professional Talk)

### FOR MEETINGS:
1. "From my experience, we should prioritize critical user flows for automation first"
2. "I believe we could improve test execution time by implementing parallel runs"
3. "I'd suggest we refactor this test module to improve maintainability"

### FOR RECOMMENDATIONS:
4. "In my opinion, we need to address flaky tests before adding more coverage"
5. "I'd recommend investing in better test reporting to improve visibility"

### FOR CONFIDENCE:
6. "I'm confident that this approach will improve our overall test stability"
7. "Based on what I've seen in the logs, the issue appears to be timing-related"

### FOR SOFTENING:
8. "It seems to me that the test failures are related to environment configuration"
9. "Perhaps we could consider using a data-driven approach for these scenarios"

### FOR STRONG POINTS:
10. "I'd argue that we should focus on reliability over coverage at this stage"
