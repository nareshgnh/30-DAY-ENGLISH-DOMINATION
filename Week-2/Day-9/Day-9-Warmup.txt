## BREATHING EXERCISE (2 min):

**Exercise 1: Confidence Breathing**
- Inhale for 4 counts: "I speak with authority"
- Hold for 4 counts: "My words carry weight"
- Exhale for 6 counts: "I express myself clearly and professionally"
- Repeat 3 times

**Exercise 2: Phrase Power Breath**
- Deep breath in
- Exhale while saying: "From my experience, to be honest, in my opinion, I'd argue that, it seems to me"
- Focus on sounding confident and natural
- Repeat 2 times

**Exercise 3: Professional Voice**
- Inhale deeply
- Exhale while saying in a strong, clear voice: "I believe the best approach is to focus on automation. From my experience, this delivers the highest ROI."
- Feel the confidence in your voice
- Repeat 2 times

## WARM-UP SENTENCES (5 min):

Read these sentences aloud 3 times each, emphasizing the phrases:

1. "From my experience, automation testing provides the best return on investment for regression testing."

2. "To be honest, I believe Playwright is superior to Selenium for modern web applications."

3. "In my opinion, test automation should focus on critical user flows rather than achieving 100% coverage."

4. "It seems to me that the best approach would be to start with API testing before moving to UI automation."

5. "I'd argue that investing time in framework architecture upfront saves significant maintenance effort later."
