## SESSION 1: RAPID-FIRE QUESTIONS (30 min)

### ChatGPT Setup Prompt:

```
For 30 minutes, ask me 40 rapid-fire questions covering Week 2 topics.

Questions should cover:
- Technical questions (frameworks, tools, testing approach)
- Behavioral questions (STAR stories)
- Opinion questions ("What do you think about...")
- Scenario questions ("How would you handle...")

Rules:
- I must answer each question in 30 seconds or less
- Judge me on: Speed, Clarity, Use of professional phrases, Correct pronunciation
- If I pause too long, say "Too slow! Next question!"
- If I use weak phrases ("I think", "but"), say "Upgrade your language!"

After 40 questions:
- Response speed score: __/10
- Professional language score: __/10
- Pronunciation clarity score: __/10
- Top 3 answers
- Top 3 answers needing improvement

Start immediately: "Question 1: What do you do? Quick 30-second answer!"
```

## SESSION 2: 10-MINUTE NON-STOP SPEECH (20 min total)

### ChatGPT Setup Prompt:

```
For this session, give me 2 topics. I'll speak non-stop for 10 minutes on each topic.

Topic 1 (10 min): "My automation testing journey and expertise"
Topic 2 (10 min): "Why I'm the right fit for a senior QA automation role"

Your role:
- DON'T interrupt
- Listen for:
  * Do I use connectors? (However, Therefore, Moreover)
  * Do I use professional phrases? (From my experience, I believe)
  * Do I think in English or translate?
  * Is pronunciation clear?
  * Do I include STAR stories?
  * Do I speak continuously without long pauses?

After each 10-minute speech:
- Fluency score: __/10
- Week 2 skills integration score: __/10
- Could I use this speech in a real interview? Yes/No
- 3 things I did excellently
- 2 things to improve

Start: "You have 10 minutes. Topic: 'My automation testing journey and expertise'. Start speaking now - don't stop until 10 minutes are up!"
```

## SESSION 3: MOCK TECHNICAL INTERVIEW (40 min)

### ChatGPT Setup Prompt:

```
For 40 minutes, conduct a comprehensive technical + behavioral interview with me.

You are: Senior Engineering Manager at a top product company
Position: Senior QA Automation Engineer (25 LPA)

Interview structure:
1. Introduction (5 min): "Tell me about yourself"
2. Technical deep-dive (15 min): Framework questions, testing approach, tools
3. Behavioral questions (15 min): 4-5 STAR questions
4. Your questions for me (5 min): I ask you questions

Evaluate:
- Overall communication quality (0-10)
- Technical clarity (0-10)
- STAR story quality (0-10)
- Professional language use (0-10)
- Pronunciation clarity (0-10)
- Would I hire this candidate? Yes/No/Maybe
- What salary range would I offer based on communication?

Be professional, take notes, ask follow-ups like a real interviewer.

Start formally: "Hello, I'm [Name], Engineering Manager. I'm excited to learn about your QA automation experience. Let's start with you telling me about yourself - your background, experience, and what you're looking for."
```
