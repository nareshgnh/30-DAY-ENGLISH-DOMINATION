## ‚ö° TOP 5 GRAMMAR RULES

**Rule 1:** Use "will" for spontaneous decisions, promises, and predictions based on opinion
- ‚úÖ I will help you debug this right now (instant decision)
- ‚úÖ I think automation will dominate testing (opinion)

**Rule 2:** Use "going to" for plans/intentions decided before speaking
- ‚úÖ I am going to learn Kubernetes next month (already planned)
- ‚úÖ I am going to apply to 50 companies (intention)

**Rule 3:** Use "going to" for predictions based on present evidence
- ‚úÖ Look at this code! It's going to crash (you can see the problem)
- ‚úÖ These tests are going to fail (evidence shows it)

**Rule 4:** Use Present Continuous (am/is/are + verb-ing) for scheduled/arranged future events
- ‚úÖ I am attending a workshop tomorrow at 3 PM (scheduled)
- ‚úÖ I am interviewing at Google next Tuesday (arranged appointment)

**Rule 5:** "will" vs "going to" - Quick test
- Decided NOW = will | Decided BEFORE = going to
- Opinion/belief = will | Plan/intention = going to
- Evidence NOW = going to | General prediction = will

## üìù QUICK GRAMMAR FORMULAS

**"will" Structure:**
- Positive: I/You/He/She/We/They + will + base verb
- Negative: Subject + will not (won't) + base verb
- Question: Will + subject + base verb?
- Example: I will learn, I won't quit, Will you help?

**"going to" Structure:**
- Positive: Subject + am/is/are + going to + base verb
- Negative: Subject + am/is/are + not going to + base verb
- Question: Am/Is/Are + subject + going to + base verb?
- Example: I am going to apply, I'm not going to give up, Are you going to attend?

**Present Continuous for Future:**
- Positive: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing (+ time reference)
- Negative: Subject + am/is/are + not + verb-ing
- Question: Am/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing?
- Example: I am attending the workshop, I'm not interviewing tomorrow, Are you meeting him?

**Decision Tree:**
- Scheduled with time? ‚Üí Present Continuous
- Already planned? ‚Üí going to
- Deciding now? ‚Üí will
- Opinion/prediction? ‚Üí will
- Evidence now? ‚Üí going to
