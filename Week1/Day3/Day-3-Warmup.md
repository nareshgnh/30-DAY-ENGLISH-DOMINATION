## WARM-UP SENTENCES (5 min):

**Read these aloud 3 times each. Notice the different future forms.**

1. I will apply to 50 companies this month.

2. I am going to prepare for interviews every single day.

3. Tomorrow, I am attending a technical workshop at 3 PM.

4. I will learn Cypress and API testing next quarter.

5. In 5 years, I will be working as a Senior QA Architect at a top product company.

**Why these sentences?** They show "will" (decisions/predictions), "going to" (plans), and present continuous for scheduled future events!

## QUICK GRAMMAR RECALL (5 min):

**Yesterday's Reminder:**
- Past Simple = specific past time ‚Üí "I worked at TCS in 2019"
- Present Perfect = life experience / duration ‚Üí "I have worked in QA for 6 years"
- Past Continuous = background action ‚Üí "I was testing when..."
