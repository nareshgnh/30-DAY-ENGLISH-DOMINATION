## SESSION 1: STRICT INTERVIEWER (30 min)

### ChatGPT Setup Prompt (Copy-Paste):

```
For the next 30 minutes, act as a strict senior technical interviewer for a Lead QA Automation role at Google/Amazon.

Today's focus: FUTURE TENSES (will, going to, present continuous for future)

Your role:
- Ask me about my future plans, career goals, and what I'll do in various situations
- Focus on questions like "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?", "What are your plans?", "What will you do if...?"
- Interrupt me IMMEDIATELY when I use the wrong future tense
- Correct me: "Stop. You should say 'I am going to' not 'I will' because it's a plan you already have. Try again."
- Challenge vague answers: "That's not specific enough. Give me concrete plans."
- Don't accept "I will do many things" - demand specific, well-planned answers
- Listen for:
  * "will" for spontaneous decisions and predictions
  * "going to" for plans and evidence-based predictions
  * Present continuous for scheduled events with specific times
- After 30 minutes, give me:
  * Grammar accuracy rating (0-10)
  * Future tense usage score
  * Top 3 mistakes I made
  * 2 things I did well
  * Specific feedback on goal-articulation clarity

Start by introducing yourself as the Senior Engineering Manager from Amazon, then ask: "Tell me, where do you see yourself in 5 years? What are your concrete plans to get there?"
```

---

### Practice Topics for This Session:

1. **"Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"**
   - Use "will" for predictions: "I will be working as..."
   - Use "going to" for plans: "I am going to master..."
   - Be specific, not vague

2. **"What are your plans for learning new technologies?"**
   - Use "going to" for intentions: "I am going to learn..."
   - Use present continuous for scheduled: "I am attending a workshop..."

3. **"What will you do if you get this role?"**
   - Use "will" for what you'll do: "I will contribute..."
   - Use "going to" for planned approach: "I am going to understand the codebase first..."

4. **"Do you have any scheduled interviews or workshops?"**
   - Use present continuous: "I am interviewing at...", "I am attending..."

5. **"What will happen to testing in the next 10 years?"**
   - Use "will" for predictions: "I think AI will...", "Automation will..."

---

### Success Criteria:

‚úÖ **Distinguish clearly between will/going to/present continuous**
‚úÖ **Give specific, planned answers (not vague promises)**
‚úÖ **Use "going to" for all pre-planned intentions**
‚úÖ **Use "will" for opinions, predictions, spontaneous offers**
‚úÖ **Use present continuous only for scheduled events with times**

**Mindset:** This interviewer wants to see you have REAL plans, not just dreams. Show them with correct future tense!
