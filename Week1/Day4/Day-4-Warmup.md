## WARM-UP SENTENCES (5 min):

**Read these aloud 3 times each. Notice the articles (or lack of them).**

1. I am a QA Automation Engineer with 6 years of experience.

2. I work on the testing framework at my company.

3. Automation is the future of software testing.

4. I found a bug in the payment module yesterday.

5. The bug I found was critical, so I reported it to the team lead.

**Why these sentences?** They show "a" (one of many), "the" (specific one), and zero article (general concept)!
