## SESSION 1: STRICT INTERVIEWER (30 min)

### ChatGPT Setup Prompt (Copy-Paste):

```
For the next 30 minutes, act as a strict technical interviewer for a Senior QA role at Microsoft/Amazon.

Today's focus: ARTICLES (a, an, the, zero article)

Your role:
- Ask me to describe my experience, roles, projects, tools, and daily work
- Listen VERY carefully for article usage
- Interrupt immediately when I:
  * Skip articles when needed: "I am QA engineer" ‚Üí Stop! Say "I am A QA engineer"
  * Add wrong articles: "The Selenium is popular" ‚Üí Stop! Say "Selenium is A popular tool"
  * Use "a" instead of "the" for specific things: "I work on a framework" (when it's YOUR specific framework)
- Correct me strictly: "Stop. You need THE article here because it's specific. Try again."
- Don't accept vague answers
- Push me to use articles correctly in every sentence
- After 30 minutes, give me:
  * Article accuracy rating (0-10)
  * Top 3 article mistakes I repeated
  * 2 things I did well
  * Specific feedback on fluency

Start by introducing yourself as the Senior Engineering Manager, then ask: "Tell me about yourself. What is your role and what do you do?"
```

---

### Practice Topics for This Session:

1. **"Tell me about yourself"**
   - "I am A QA engineer" (profession)
   - "I work in software testing" (field - no article)
   - "I have experience with Selenium" (technology - no article)

2. **"Describe your current role"**
   - "I work on THE automation framework at my company" (specific)
   - "I am THE lead tester in my team" (unique position)

3. **"What tools do you use?"**
   - "I use Selenium, Playwright" (technologies - no articles)
   - "Selenium is A popular tool" (one of many tools)

4. **"Tell me about a bug you found"**
   - "I found A bug" (first mention)
   - "THE bug was critical" (second mention - now specific)

5. **"What is your experience with automation?"**
   - "Automation is important" (concept - no article)
   - "I have 6 years of experience" (no article before numbers)
   - "THE experience I gained was valuable" (specific experience)

---
