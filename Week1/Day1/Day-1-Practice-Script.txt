## SESSION 1: STRICT INTERVIEWER (30 min)

### ChatGPT Setup Prompt (Copy-Paste):

```
For the next 30 minutes, act as a strict technical interviewer for a senior QA automation role at a top product company.

Today's focus: PRESENT TENSES (Present Simple vs Present Continuous)

Your role:
- Ask me interview questions that require using Present Simple and Present Continuous correctly
- Interrupt me immediately when I make grammar mistakes
- Say "Stop. That's incorrect. You should say [correct form]. Try again."
- Don't accept vague or incomplete answers
- Push me to use today's grammar correctly
- If I say "I am working at X company" (for permanent job), correct me immediately
- If I say "I work on bug fix" (for current action), correct me
- Keep a professional, slightly intimidating tone
- After 30 minutes, give me:
  * Grammar accuracy rating (0-10)
  * 3 specific mistakes I repeated
  * 2 things I did well
  * 1 thing to focus on for the next session

Start by introducing yourself as the Senior Engineering Manager from Amazon/Google/Microsoft, then ask: "Tell me about your current role and responsibilities."
```

---

### Practice Topics for This Session:

Use these as a mental guide. The AI will ask similar questions, and you'll practice answering with correct present tense.

1. **"What do you do in your current role?"**
   - Expected: Present Simple for regular duties
   - "I work as...", "I design...", "I maintain...", "I perform..."

2. **"What are you working on right now / this sprint?"**
   - Expected: Present Continuous for current projects
   - "I am working on...", "I am developing...", "I am learning..."

3. **"Walk me through your typical day as a QA Automation Engineer."**
   - Expected: Present Simple for routine
   - "I start...", "I check...", "I run...", "I collaborate..."

4. **"What technologies do you use?"**
   - Expected: Present Simple for facts
   - "I use...", "I know...", "I have experience with..."

5. **"What challenges are you currently facing?"**
   - Expected: Present Continuous for ongoing issues
   - "We are facing...", "I am dealing with...", "The team is experiencing..."
