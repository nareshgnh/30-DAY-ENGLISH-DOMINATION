## ğŸ“ QUICK GRAMMAR FORMULAS

**Present Simple:**
- Positive: I/You/We/They + verb | He/She/It + verb-s/es
- Negative: I/You/We/They + don't + verb | He/She/It + doesn't + verb
- Question: Do + I/you/we/they + verb? | Does + he/she/it + verb?

**Present Continuous:**
- Positive: I am, You/We/They are, He/She/It is + verb-ing
- Negative: I am not, You/We/They aren't, He/She/It isn't + verb-ing
- Question: Am I, Are you/we/they, Is he/she/it + verb-ing?

**Keywords:**
- Present Simple: every day, usually, always, often, sometimes, never
- Present Continuous: now, right now, at the moment, currently, today, this week, these days
