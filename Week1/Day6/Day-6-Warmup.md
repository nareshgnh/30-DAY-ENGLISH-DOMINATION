## WARM-UP SENTENCES (5 min):

**Read these aloud 3 times each. Notice the auxiliaries.**

1. He doesn't know Selenium, but I do.

2. She has 6 years of experience, and I have 5.

3. They are testing the application, and we are reviewing the results.

4. I did the testing yesterday, and he did the deployment.

5. It was challenging, but we were prepared.

**Why these sentences?** They show correct usage of do/does, have/has, is/are/was/were!
