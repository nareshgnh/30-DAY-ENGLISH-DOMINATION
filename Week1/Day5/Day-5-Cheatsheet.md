## üìù QUICK PREPOSITION FORMULAS

**TIME RULE:**
- IN ‚Üí longer periods (months, years, seasons)
  - in January, in 2020, in the morning
- ON ‚Üí specific days and dates
  - on Monday, on the 15th, on weekends
- AT ‚Üí clock times and moments
  - at 3 PM, at midnight, at noon

**PLACE RULE:**
- IN ‚Üí enclosed spaces (cities, countries, rooms)
  - in Bangalore, in India, in the office
- ON ‚Üí surfaces and floors
  - on MG Road, on the 5th floor, on the wall
- AT ‚Üí specific points and locations
  - at TCS, at home, at the airport

**OTHER PATTERNS:**
- good/skilled/expert AT (skill)
- work AT (company) / work IN (city/field)
- finish BY (deadline)
- work FOR (duration)
- work WITH (tools)
- learn ABOUT (topic)
- FROM...TO (range)

**MEMORY TRICK:**
"BIG ‚Üí SMALL for time and place"
IN (big container) ‚Üí ON (surface) ‚Üí AT (specific point)
