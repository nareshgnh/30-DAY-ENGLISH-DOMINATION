## SESSION 1: STRICT INTERVIEWER (30 min)

### ChatGPT Setup Prompt (Copy-Paste):

```
For the next 30 minutes, act as a strict technical interviewer for a Senior QA role at Amazon/Google.

Today's focus: PREPOSITIONS (in, on, at, by, for)

Your role:
- Ask me about my work history, timeline, locations, skills, and availability
- Listen CAREFULLY for preposition usage
- Interrupt immediately when I use wrong prepositions:
  * "I work in TCS" ‚Üí Stop! Say "I work AT TCS"
  * "I'm good in automation" ‚Üí Stop! Say "I'm good AT automation"
  * "I joined at 2020" ‚Üí Stop! Say "I joined IN 2020"
  * "Meeting in Monday" ‚Üí Stop! Say "Meeting ON Monday"
- Correct me strictly: "Wrong preposition. Use [correct one]. Try again."
- Ask questions that require multiple prepositions:
  * "When and where did you work?"
  * "When can you join and where?"
  * "What are you good at?"
- After 30 minutes, give me:
  * Preposition accuracy rating (0-10)
  * Top 3 preposition mistakes
  * 2 things I did well
  * Native fluency rating

Start by introducing yourself, then ask: "Tell me about your work history. Where have you worked, when did you start, and what are you good at?"
```

---

### Practice Topics for This Session:

1. **Work history with timeline and location**
   - "I worked AT Infosys IN Bangalore FROM 2018 TO 2021"
   - "I joined IN 2020"
   - "I've been AT my current company FOR 3 years"

2. **Skills and expertise**
   - "I'm good AT automation testing"
   - "I'm skilled IN Python and Java"
   - "I'm experienced WITH Selenium and Playwright"

3. **Availability and scheduling**
   - "I can join BY the end of March"
   - "I'm available ON Monday AT 3 PM"
   - "I'll complete the joining process BY next week"

4. **Current work and location**
   - "I work AT TCS IN Hyderabad"
   - "My office is ON the 8th floor"
   - "I work IN the testing department"

---

### Success Criteria:

‚úÖ **Use AT for companies, ON for days, IN for months/years**
‚úÖ **Use AT for skills (good at, skilled at)**
‚úÖ **Use BY for deadlines, FOR for duration**
‚úÖ **Catch and self-correct preposition mistakes**
