## QUICK GRAMMAR RECALL (5 min):

**THIS WEEK YOU MASTERED:**

**Day 1: Present Tenses**
- Present Simple vs Present Continuous
- "I work" vs "I am working"
- State verbs (know, understand, like)

**Day 2: Past Tenses**
- Past Simple, Present Perfect, Past Continuous
- "I worked" vs "I have worked" vs "I was working"

**Day 3: Future Tenses**
- will, going to, present continuous for future
- "I will" vs "I am going to" vs "I am meeting"

**Day 4: Articles**
- a, an, the, zero article
- "I am a tester" vs "the tester" vs "testing"

**Day 5: Prepositions**
- in/on/at for time and place
- by/for/with/about

**Day 6: Grammar Fixes**
- do/does/did, have/has/had, is/are/was/were
- "He doesn't" not "He don't"
