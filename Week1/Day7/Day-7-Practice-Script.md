```

- Conduct a FULL technical interview covering:
  * My background and experience (tenses, prepositions)

  * Future goals (future tenses)
  * My skills (auxiliaries, prepositions)

  * Top 5 mistakes I made
  * Top 3 strengths
  * Overall interview impression
  * Would you hire me? Why/why not?

```

### What This Session Tests:

**ALL Week 1 Topics in One Interview:**

1. **Self-introduction:** Present tenses, articles, prepositions
   - "I am a QA engineer at TCS in Bangalore"
   - "I have 6 years of experience"
   - "I'm good at automation testing"

2. **Past experience:** Past tenses, prepositions
   - "I worked at Wipro from 2018 to 2021"
   - "I was the lead QA engineer"
   - "I have worked on multiple projects"

3. **Current work:** Present continuous, articles
   - "I am working on the automation framework"
   - "Currently, I am learning Kubernetes"

4. **Future plans:** Future tenses, time prepositions
   - "I am going to master cloud testing"
   - "I will contribute to your team"
   - "I am interviewing with other companies on Monday at 3 PM"

5. **Team description:** Auxiliaries (he/she/they)
   - "My manager doesn't know Python"
   - "She has 10 years of experience"
   - "They are working on a new project"
