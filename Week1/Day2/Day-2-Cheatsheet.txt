## âœ… SELF-CHECK (Did I Learn Today's Topic?)

1. **Can I explain when to use Past Simple vs Present Perfect?**
   - Past Simple: specific time (I worked there in 2019)
   - Present Perfect: no specific time / connection to now (I have worked there for 5 years)

2. **Do I know when to use "since" vs "in"?**
   - Since = Present Perfect (I have worked since 2020)
   - In = Past Simple (I worked in 2020)

3. **Can I tell a STAR story using correct past tenses?**
   - Try: "Last year, I worked on X project. I was responsible for Y. I faced challenge Z. I implemented solution A, B, C, and delivered results."

4. **Do I avoid saying "I am working since..."?**
   - Always: "I have worked since..." or "I have been working since..."

5. **Can I use Past Continuous for background actions?**
   - "I was testing when I found the bug" - Yes!

**If you answered YES to all 5, you're ready for Day 3!**
