## BREATHING EXERCISE (2 min):

**Exercise 1: Power Breath (30 seconds)**
- Inhale for 4 counts
- Hold for 4 counts
- Exhale for 6 counts
- Repeat while saying: "If I were in the interview right now, I would speak with confidence."

**Exercise 2: Confidence Anchor (30 seconds)**
- Take 3 deep breaths
- With each exhale, say:
  * "I have practiced for 21 days"
  * "I am interview-ready"
  * "I will communicate like a senior engineer"

**Exercise 3: Advanced Speaker Breath (1 minute)**
- Breathe normally
- Say these complex sentences smoothly:
  * "If I had known about automation earlier, I would have started sooner"
  * "If I were the tech lead, I would implement comprehensive test coverage"
  * "If I could redesign the framework, I would focus on scalability first"
