## BREATHING EXERCISE (2 min):

**Exercise 1: Speaker's Breath (30 seconds)**
- Stand up (yes, physically stand!)
- Inhale deeply for 5 counts
- Hold for 3 counts
- Exhale while saying loudly: "Today I will present my framework like a professional speaker."
- Repeat 3 times with power and confidence

**Exercise 2: Voice Projection Prep (30 seconds)**
- Take a deep breath
- Say these phrases with increasing volume:
  * (Normal) "First, I will explain the architecture."
  * (Louder) "Next, I will discuss the implementation."
  * (Loudest) "Finally, I will show you the results!"

**Exercise 3: Presentation Confidence Breath (1 minute)**
- Breathe normally
- With each exhale, say confidently:
  * "I am ready to present"
  * "My ideas are valuable"
  * "I will speak clearly and confidently"
  * "I command attention when I speak"

## WARM-UP SENTENCES (5 min):

**Read these aloud 3 times each, as if presenting to an audience:**

1. "Good morning everyone. Today I'm excited to present our new automation framework architecture. First, I'll explain the problem we were facing. Next, I'll walk you through our solution. Finally, I'll share the impressive results we've achieved."

2. "Let me start by giving you some context. Our testing process had been inefficient for months. To address this, we designed a modular framework. Moving on to the technical details, I'll show you how we implemented page object model with Playwright."

3. "I'd like to draw your attention to this particular aspect of our framework. As you can see, we've achieved 80% test coverage. What's particularly interesting is how we reduced execution time by 60%. Let me explain how we accomplished this."

4. "To summarize what we've discussed so far: we identified the problem, we designed the solution, and we implemented it successfully. Now, let's move on to the Q&A session. I'm happy to answer any questions you might have."

5. "In conclusion, this framework has transformed our testing process. The key takeaways are: first, modularity enables scalability; second, proper architecture reduces maintenance; and third, automation saves significant time and resources. Thank you for your attention."
