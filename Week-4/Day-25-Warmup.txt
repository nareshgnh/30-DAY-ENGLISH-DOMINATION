## BREATHING EXERCISE (2 min):

**Exercise 1: Calm Under Pressure (1 minute)**
- 4-7-8 Breathing (anxiety reduction technique)
- Inhale through nose: 4 counts
- Hold: 7 counts
- Exhale through mouth: 8 counts
- Repeat 4 times while thinking: "I am calm. I am confident. I am ready."

**Exercise 2: Power Posture + Breath (1 minute)**
- Stand in power pose (hands on hips, chest out)
- Take 5 deep breaths
- With each exhale say: "Pressure makes me sharper, not weaker."
