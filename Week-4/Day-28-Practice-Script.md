## SESSION 2: ELIMINATE FILLER WORDS (30 min)

```

```

**Success Criteria:**
✅ Less than 5 filler words in 30 minutes
✅ Natural strategic pauses instead
✅ Comfortable with silence

## SESSION 4: SALARY NEGOTIATION MASTERY (20 min)

```

Your role:
- Ask: "What are your salary expectations?"
- I'll give my answer
- Push back hard: "That's too high", "We budgeted less", "You lack experience for that"
- Test if I stay confident and justify well
- After 20 minutes: Am I ready to negotiate 25 LPA confidently?

```

**Success Criteria:**
✅ State 23-25 LPA confidently
✅ Justify with value and data
✅ Stay calm under pushback
✅ Sound like peer negotiation (not desperation)
