**Rule 1: STRUCTURE EVERYTHING (Hook-Body-Conclusion)**
‚úÖ "What if testing could be 70% faster? [Hook] Let me explain three key strategies. [Body] To summarize: we transformed our testing. [Conclusion]"

**Rule 2: SIGNPOST CONSTANTLY**
‚úÖ "First... Moving on to... What's particularly interesting is... Finally... To summarize..."

**Rule 3: ENGAGE YOUR AUDIENCE**
‚úÖ "You might be wondering why... As you can see... Let me show you... Imagine..."

**Rule 4: PAUSE, DON'T FILL**
‚úÖ "We achieved three major outcomes. [PAUSE] First, speed. [PAUSE] Second, reliability. [PAUSE] Third, cost savings."

**Rule 5: HANDLE Q&A PROFESSIONALLY (ACAC)**
‚úÖ "Great question [Acknowledge]. Just to clarify... [Clarify]. Here's the answer... [Answer]. Does that help? [Confirm]"
