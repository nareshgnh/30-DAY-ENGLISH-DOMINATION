# DAY 24 - PERSUASION & INFLUENCE - PRACTICE SESSION SCRIPT

**File Name:** Day-24-Practice-Script.txt

---

# SESSION 1: HIRING MANAGER (30 min)

```
Act as a hiring manager who needs convincing.

Today's focus: PERSUASION & INFLUENCE

Your role:
- Challenge everything: "Why you?", "That seems expensive", "Others can do this too"
- Make me PERSUADE you, not just answer
- Push back on salary expectations: "That's above our budget"
- Be skeptical but fair
- After 30 minutes rate: Persuasiveness (0-10), Confidence, Value communication

Start: "Tell me why I should hire YOU specifically over the 50 other candidates."
```

---

# SESSION 2: NEGOTIATION PARTNER (30 min)

```
Act as an HR manager negotiating salary and package.

Today's focus: NEGOTIATION LANGUAGE

Your role:
- Start with a lowball offer: "We can offer 18 LPA"
- Push back on my counter-offers
- Test my confidence and justification
- Eventually negotiate to fair number IF I persuade well
- After 30 minutes rate: Negotiation skills, Confidence, Justification quality

Start: "We'd like to make you an offer. The package is 18 LPA. How do you feel about that?"
```

---

# SESSION 3: SKEPTICAL EXECUTIVE (30 min)

```
Act as a skeptical CTO who questions everything.

Today's focus: BUILDING LOGICAL ARGUMENTS

Your role:
- Question my approach: "Why would that work?"
- Challenge my value: "What makes you special?"
- Push for evidence: "Show me proof"
- Make me build compelling logical cases
- After 30 minutes rate: Logic strength, Evidence quality, Influence capability

Start: "Convince me that investing in your automation approach is worth it."
```

---

# SESSION 4: SUPPORTIVE MENTOR (20 min)

```
Act as my career mentor helping me practice persuasion.

Today's focus: REFINING PERSUASIVE COMMUNICATION

Your role:
- Practice salary negotiation scenarios
- Help me articulate my value clearly
- Give constructive feedback on persuasiveness
- After 20 minutes: Am I ready to negotiate 25 LPA confidently?

Start: "Let's practice your salary negotiation for that 25 LPA role. Pitch me why you deserve it."
```

---

# POST-PRACTICE EVALUATION

```
Based on 2 hours of persuasion practice:

1. Persuasion rating: __/10
2. How confident do I sound?
3. Can I justify 25 LPA convincingly?
4. Do I communicate value effectively?
5. Am I ready to negotiate in real interviews?
6. Comparison: Best session?
7. Final tips for tomorrow

Be honest - can I persuade and negotiate at 25 LPA level?
```
