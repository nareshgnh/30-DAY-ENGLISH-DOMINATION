## FIX AREA 4: SALARY NEGOTIATION (15 min)

**If salary discussion was weak:**

**RECONSTRUCT SALARY ANSWER:**

**Q: "What are your salary expectations?"**

**Perfect Answer Structure:**
1. Acknowledgment: "Thank you for asking."
2. State range confidently: "Based on my 6 years of experience and market research, I'm targeting 23-25 LPA."
3. Justify with value: "This range reflects the specialized skills I bring and the measurable impact I've delivered - specifically [concrete examples]."
4. Show flexibility: "I'm open to discussing the complete package including [learning, growth, etc.]"

**PRACTICE:**
- Say this 20 times
- Practice with different objections:
  * "That's too high"
  * "We budgeted 18 LPA"
  * "You lack experience for that range"
