## PART 1: COMPLETE SKILLS INVENTORY (30 min)

### WEEK 1: FOUNDATION - Grammar Fundamentals

**Core Skills Learned:**
1. **Present Tenses** (Day 1)
   - Present Simple: "I work on automation testing"
   - Present Continuous: "I am currently working on a framework"
   - When to use each

2. **Past Tenses** (Day 2)
   - Past Simple: "I completed the project"
   - Present Perfect: "I have worked on 15 projects"
   - Past Continuous: "I was working when..."
   - Difference and usage

3. **Future Tenses** (Day 3)
   - Will: "I will complete this by Friday"
   - Going to: "I'm going to implement this feature"
   - Present continuous for future: "I'm meeting the stakeholders tomorrow"

4. **Articles** (Day 4)
   - a, an, the, zero article
   - Rules and common mistakes

5. **Prepositions** (Day 5)
   - Time: in the morning, on Monday, at 5pm
   - Place: in Bangalore, at the office, on the floor
   - Movement: to, from, through

6. **Common Grammar Fixes** (Day 6)
   - do/does/did
   - have/has/had
   - is/are/was/were
   - Subject-verb agreement

7. **Sentence Structure** (Day 7)
   - Subject + Verb + Object
   - Complex sentences with because, although, when
